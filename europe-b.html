Format

Swiss Stage:16 Team Swiss System Format

  • Elimination and Advancement matches are Bo3
  • All other matches are Bo1
  • The Buchholz system is used to seed matches from Round 3 to 5
  • 3rd place decider is resolved via a Bo3 single-elimination match
  • Top 8 Teams proceed to the PGL Major Antwerp
  • Bottom 8 Teams are eliminated
